A contemporary strategic partner for elevated world class brands.

Focus on fashion industry for 20 years.

Lloyd is your one-stop-resource for Fashion Apparel. We focus on trending categories such as Intimate Apparel, Dresses, Loungewear, Active wear, Athleisure and Streetwear. Menswear and Childrenswear are available as well.

People

They identify and apply relevant trend concepts that align with your customer and brand vision.
